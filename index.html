About the Initiative

The Centre for Research in Tamil Nadu (CRTN) is a socio-political research organization dedicated to advancing critical scholarship that responds to the social, economic, and cultural dynamics of Tamil Nadu.

CRTN brings together a dedicated network of scholars whose research explores and addresses the evolving realities of the State, with a strong commitment to social equity and public welfare. Our core purpose is to bridge academic research with grassroots realities—ensuring that the knowledge generated contributes meaningfully to inclusive development, democratic participation, and informed social policy.

Vision

To be a leading research network in Tamil Nadu that drives impactful, ethically grounded, and socially responsible research commitments, enabling equitable progress and fostering a future where knowledge strengthens democracy and public wellbeing.

Mission

  • To nurture a dynamic community of research scholars committed to advancing evidence-based insights and progressive public discourse.
  • To promote rigorous, interdisciplinary, and socially engaged research addressing the needs and aspirations of the people of Tamil Nadu.
  • To establish strong linkages between academic research, civil society, and policy institutions for enhanced societal impact.
  • To create platforms for collaboration between folk lore researchers, community knowledge holders, and academic researchers, ensuring participatory and inclusive knowledge generation.

Core Objectives

Knowledge

Knowledge Production

Advance high-quality research in governance, labour, human rights, agrarian sector, environmental sustainability, social justice, language, anthropological, historical and cultural development, contemporary political discussions, gender and Dalit studies in Tamil Nadu.

Capacity

Research Capacity Development

Empower emerging researchers through mentorship, workshops, and collaborative learning platforms.

Policy

Policy Engagement

Deliver analytically sound recommendations that support equitable development policies and strengthen participatory decision-making.

Archive

Documentation & Archiving

Develop comprehensive repositories chronicling data sets on different disciplines, public movements, socio-economic challenges, innovations, and community-driven solutions across the State.

Outreach

Public Outreach & Dissemination

Enhance public understanding through journals, seminars, public lectures, policy briefs, dialogues, round table discussions, and digital knowledge platforms.

Partners

Partnerships & Collaboration

Build sustainable networks with universities, think tanks, social organizations, and professional bodies working towards collective social advancement.

Organizational Culture

CRTN fosters a culture founded on:

  • Integrity and Scientific Excellence
  • Inclusivity and Diversity of Thought
  • Respectful Collaboration and Shared Learning
  • Ethical, Transparent, and Responsible Research Practices
  • People-Centric and Purpose-Driven Knowledge

We recognize research as a catalyst for progress and ensure that our work contributes to reducing inequalities in research and enhancing a multi-disciplinary network in research.

Organizational Structure

Advisory Board

Comprising senior academicians, experts, thought leaders, and practitioners providing strategic guidance, research quality review, and institutional direction.

Working Group

Responsible for programme execution, publications, research facilitation, community liaison, and organizational development.

Members

Academicians and research scholars conducting Tamil Nadu-focused studies that align with CRTN's goals of promoting social and developmental advancement.

Collaborative Partners

Universities, research centres, civil society networks, public institutions, publications and knowledge communities contributing expertise and shared resources.
